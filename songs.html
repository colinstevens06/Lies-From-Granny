Lies From Granny

Song List

We're always adding new songs to our binder. We try to pull from our influences but always with an ear to what our crowds will sing and dance along to.

Pulling inspiration from a number of fock genres, we play songs that span several decades, back to the Beatles in the '60s up to some more recent jams.

Our bread and butter: pop punk.

Have ideas for a song we'd kill? Hit us up on Instagram or Twitter.

Want to book us for a gig? We'll work with you to build a setlist for your audience. Hit us up about a booking.
